Frequently Asked Questions

Who is ArogyaMitra for?

ArogyaMitra is specifically designed to provide essential health information to individuals in rural and semi-urban communities across India.

How does ArogyaMitra get its information?

The chatbot is integrated with official government health databases to ensure all information about diseases, vaccinations, and alerts is accurate and reliable.

What languages does the chatbot support?

The chatbot is multilingual and currently supports Hindi, English, Bengali, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, Malayalam, Hinglish, and Odia to serve a wide range of communities.

Can I use ArogyaMitra for medical emergencies?

**No, ArogyaMitra is not a substitute for professional medical advice.** In case of a medical emergency, please contact your doctor or dial your local emergency services immediately.

How accurate is the information?

Our system aims to achieve at least 80% accuracy in answering health queries by using verified data from government health databases.

Is there a mobile app?

Currently, ArogyaMitra is a web-based service accessible on all devices. We are exploring the development of a dedicated mobile app in the future.
